## 🎤 Interview Prep Features

### Question Bank (10 Curated Questions)
**Categories:**
- Behavioral (tell me about yourself, handling priorities)
- Technical (AI systems, cloud infrastructure, staying current)
- System Design (distributed systems for 30K agents)
- Leadership (team challenges, project leadership)
- Sales (closing deals, CRM automation)

### AI Feedback Engine
**Analyzes:**
- Answer length (100-200 words recommended)
- Use of quantifiable metrics
- STAR method structure
- Company/experience mentions
- Technical vocabulary depth

**Grades:**
- A (80-100): Excellent, ready for interview
- B (60-79): Good, minor tweaks needed
- C (40-59): Keep practicing, focus on metrics
- D (0-39): Needs significant improvement

**Features:**
- Built-in tips for each question
- Voice recording simulation (UI ready)
- Filter by difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Company-specific research (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Meta)
